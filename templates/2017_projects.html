More Advanced Gripper Arm

August 2017

A more advanced electronic version of the robotic gripper arm which was operated by stepper motors and Arudino. The task remained the same; to pick up a medium sized metal ring. Used a unique gripper design instead of the closing grippers. The gripper set into the centre of the ring and two prongs would unfolded to lift the ring. Unfortunately this was before I even had a smartphone so no images of this could be found.
